Rules of the game...

1. Rock beats scissor.., Scissor beats paper.., Paper beats rock.
(e.g if you pick scissor and computer picks paper,
you wins but if computer picks rock while you pick scissor computer wins.)

2. First to score 20 points wins.

3. Game ends when one of the players scores 20 points or on timeout.

4. Game time is 2mins.

5. Computer automatically wins on timeout.
